ACCOUNT DELETION — Guideline 5.1.1(v)
Settings (设置) → 账户 (Account) → 删除账户 (Delete Account). After a
confirmation dialog, the server permanently erases ALL of the user's data —
recordings, articles, photos, settings, community posts, public share links,
and the Sign-in-with-Apple binding — and the app clears all local data and
returns to a brand-new empty state. The flow is fully in-app, immediate, and
irreversible (no deactivation, no customer-service step).

BACKGROUND AUDIO — Guideline 2.5.4
VoiceDrop is a voice-dictation recorder. The UIBackgroundModes "audio" key is
required for background RECORDING (a documented use of this mode): users start
a recording and then lock the screen or leave the app while continuing to
speak — often for many minutes of hands-free dictation. The app also exposes a
"Start recording" App Shortcut (lock-screen / Action-button) that records with
the screen locked. Without the audio background mode, recordings would be cut
off the moment the phone locks. A screen recording demonstrating recording
continuing on the Home Screen / lock screen is attached.

USER-GENERATED CONTENT — Guideline 1.2 compliance (age rating set to 18+)
"VD社区" (VD Community) lets users share their own articles publicly. All
required safeguards are implemented:

1) Filtering of objectionable content (before it is posted): when a user shares
   an article to the community, the server automatically screens the full text
   and rejects the share if objectionable content (sexual, violent, hateful,
   harassing, illegal, self-harm, etc.) is detected.

2) Report mechanism + timely action: every community post has a "举报" (Report)
   action in its ⋯ menu. Reporting IMMEDIATELY removes the post from the public
   feed (hidden pending review) — not just down-ranked. Reports go to an internal
   moderation queue that the operator reviews and acts on within 24 hours
   (permanent removal of the content and ejection of the offending user).

3) Block abusive users: every post's ⋯ menu has "屏蔽此用户" (Block this user).
   A blocked author's content is hidden from that user everywhere. Blocked users
   can be managed/unblocked in Settings → 已屏蔽用户.

4) Users can immediately remove posts from the feed: reporting hides any post
   instantly, and owners can 取消分享 (Unshare) their own posts at any time.

5) Published contact + EULA with zero tolerance: before a user's FIRST community
   post, they must agree to a community agreement (社区公约 / EULA) that states a
   zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content and abusive behavior. The
   agreement and a "联系我们 / 内容投诉" (Contact / Report content) link are in
   Settings; content concerns reach us at jianshuo@hotmail.com.

To test: open the VD社区 tab, open any post, tap ⋯ to see Report / Block. The
agree-gate appears the first time you toggle "VD社区可见" on a recording.
Account deletion: 设置 → 账户 → 删除账户.
